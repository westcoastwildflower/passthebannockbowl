How do you like your bannock?

A big bowl of Indigenous written/themed media content

Do you use margarine? Do you like raisins Do you like it with butter and honey? Peanut butter and jam? What brings back the best memories? Here you will find a link to make your own!


We invite you to make some tea and explore through your fingertips!


Disclaimer:This site will be updated monthly. Ongoing events may not be reflected in the page's content yet.

Lisa Wilson

Content Manager

Lisa has a diploma in Hospitality Management, and more recently, a certificate in Foundations in Technology. She shares a passion for helping Indigenous people create space in the digital realm by helping tell their stories through digital media.

Lisa has worked with another community-based business Taxumajehjeh, where she is a team member and facilitator of Kairos Blanket Exercise. Also, Lisa has worked within her community as a research assistant.

This was created for an assingment and will continue to be a labour of love as she continues to her web development skills.

Pass the Bannock Bowl

Food for Thought

We aim to introduce you to various written content, digital media produced by Indigenous people. We hope to fill your mind with good medicine. Get cozy and ready food for thought-inducing digital spaces, and be prepared for questions to ponder and answers you didn't know you needed. Happy Scrolling cousins!

We hope to encourage those conversations that are difficult to navigate because we feel so deeply about the topics, and afterwards, we may not come out with maybe not a direct answer. Perhaps we come out with another layer of understanding and compassion.
